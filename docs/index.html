Chris Krycho

Creativity, reﬂection, & passionate endeavors by a peculiar fellow

I aim to glorify God ardently, love my wife and daughter passionately, serve my local church energetically, and fulfill my responsibilities enthusiastically. In short, to spend my days well.

  • Designgineering – in which I plunge into web design and back end development: work I can delight in.
  • Ardent Fidelity – in which I meditate on what it means to follow Jesus Christ, review books, post quotes, and share the fruits of academia.
  • Ars Artis – in which I am all over the map: I write poetry, I muse on the muses, I compose for orchestras, I post pictures.
  • From the Hearth – in which I keep my family happy with updates, pictures, and videos of our growing family.
